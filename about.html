OUR STORY

Plant Palette was founded in the heart of Ottawa by James Carter, a passionate chef and advocate for plant-based living. Inspired by the vibrant colors and flavors of nature, James envisioned a restaurant that would not only serve delicious vegetarian and vegan dishes but also foster a community centered on health, sustainability, and compassion.

After years of experimenting with plant-based recipes and traveling to explore global cuisines, James brought his dream to life in the form of Plant Palette.

Every dish on the menu reflects his commitment to using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and his belief that plant-based meals can be both exciting and satisfying. Today, Plant Palette stands as a welcoming haven for everyone—from lifelong vegans to curious foodies—offering a menu that delights the senses and nurtures the soul. Join us at Plant Palette and experience the vibrant world of plant-based dining!

TEAM MEMBERS

James Carter - Owner & Visionary

James Carter, the founder of Plant Palette, is passionate about creating a welcoming space for plant-based food lovers. His vision is rooted in sustainability and a love for vibrant, healthy meals. James ensures every detail of Plant Palette reflects his commitment to quality and community.

Emma Rivera - Head Chef

Emma Rivera brings creativity and expertise to the kitchen, crafting innovative plant-based dishes that delight every palate. With a decade of culinary experience, Emma's passion for bold flavors and fresh ingredients shines through in every dish she creates.
